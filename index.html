About:

The inspiration behind this project is derived from market trends, user experience, and content monetization. The success of subscription-based services like Spotify and Apple Music shows market viability. Offering tailored playlists and recommendations based on user preferences with the provision of easy access to a vast library of music anytime, anywhere will enable users to discover new artists, genres, and tracks. Free streaming with ads can attract a large user base while exclusive releases and partnerships with artists can attract subscribers. (This is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School.)
